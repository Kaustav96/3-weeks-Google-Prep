To count primes in range of queries -> brute force is to take every number in ranger [l,r] and check for prime. This can lead to TLE. Use Sieve of Eratosthenes logic to get the prime numbers. The sieve of Eratosthenes is one of the most efficient ways to find all primes smaller than n when n is smaller than 10 million or so We can create matrix from 2 to n. (0 is not prime & 1 is neither prime nor composite) We take 2 and mark all numbers that are visible by 2 and also divisible by square of it. Similarly we do it for next unmarke numbers. The numbers left unmarked are the prime numbers. Code -> ```void sieveOfEratosthenes(int n){ boolean[] prime = new boolean[n+1]; Arrays.fill(prime,true);// marking all as true the prime ones will be false at the end. for(int i=2;i*i<=n;i++){ if(prime[i]==true){ for (int j = i * i;j <= n; j += i) prime[j] = false; } } }```
